Rules & Strategy

Movement
  • One queen is shared between both players.
  • On your turn move the queen left, down, or diagonally down-left — any distance.
  • Moving right or up is forbidden. You must always move.
Winning

The player who moves the queen to square (0,0) — the bottom-left corner — wins.

P-positions (green)

These are safe landing squares — the player who lands there has a guaranteed win path. Coordinates: (⌊nφ⌋, ⌊nφ²⌋) for n = 0,1,2,… where φ is the golden ratio.

Tip

Start is P-position → be Player 2. Start is N-position → be Player 1 and jump to a P-position immediately.
